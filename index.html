I am a PhD student at the University of Toronto (UofT) in the Toronto Intelligent Systems Lab (TISL), advised by Igor Gilitschenski and Jonathan Kelly. Before joining UofT, I received my B.Sc. in computer engineering from Sharif University of Technology.

My research focuses on developing innovative solutions for 3D generation and editing. As a PhD student, I am working towards creating a 3D Photoshop-like tool that leverages the power of generative models to revolutionize the way we interact with 3D scenes/objects to manipulate them. With a strong belief in the intuition and creativity enabled by 3D interfaces, I aim to expand the capabilities of generative models beyond 2D image synthesis and bring them into the third dimension. My work has potential applications in various fields such as computer graphics, computer vision, robotics, and virtual reality.
